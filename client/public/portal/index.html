Nu Chapter Portal

Portal Guide

Everything you need to know about using the Nu Chapter Professional Network.

Getting Started
What is this portal?

The Nu Chapter Professional Network is a private platform for AKPsi Nu Chapter alumni and active brothers to connect, network, and support each other professionally. It is maintained by VPAR and accessible only to approved chapter members.

How do I find alumni?

Go to the Alumni Directory and search by name, company, role, field, or location. Click Contact to send a warm outreach email directly through Gmail, or click LinkedIn to view their profile.

How do I update my profile?

Go to the Alumni Directory, find your card, and click Edit My Profile. Submit your changes and VPAR will review and approve them before they go live. You can update your company, role, location, LinkedIn, pledge class, grad year, and profile photo.

How do I log out?

Click the Sign Out button in the top right of the nav. You will need to log back in with your approved Google account next time you visit.

Mentorship
How does mentorship work?

Go to Mentorship Program to browse available alumni mentors and brothers seeking mentorship. Submit a request and VPAR will review and confirm pairings. Once paired, both sides get access to a shared workspace with notes and check-in tracking.

What is My Mentorship?

If you have been matched with a mentor or mentee, a My Mentorship button will appear in the nav. This is your private workspace to track check-ins, add shared notes, and monitor progress throughout the semester.

Community
What is the DEI Hub?

The DEI Hub is a space for inclusion resources, Eye2Eye programming, chapter events, and brother feedback. The Feedback tab is only accessible to active brothers and contains anonymous forms managed by VPAR and the DEI committee.

Who do I contact for help?

Reach out to VPAR directly for any portal issues, access problems, or profile questions. For mentorship questions, contact VPAR through the portal or at your chapter meetings.
